Information Kit

826 National x Padma Lakshmi Food Writing Contest

For my final PR 209 project, I created an information kit for a campaign I ideated and created for 826 National, a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping students, ages six to 18, improve their expository and creative writing skills.

This campaign features my idea for the organization to partner with Padma Lakshmi. 826 National and Lakshmi co-host a food writing contest. Components of this information kit are as follows:

  1. (Pages 1 to 3) News Release
  2. (Pages 4) Fact Sheet
  3. (Pages 5 to 7) Q & A
  4. (Pages 8 and 9) Media Pitch
  5. (Pages 10) Biography
