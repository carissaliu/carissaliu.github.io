@CarissaEats

My biggest project to date...and one that I love so dearly.

www.carissaeats.com is a collection of my favorite places, dishes, and stories about food.

It is the companion to my Instagram page, @CarissaEats.

After my summer at Girls Who Code, I knew I loved the process behind making a website and given my love for food, I had no doubt in my mind that my first website would be a deeper dive into my food adventures. I built this blog from scratch, design and tech-wise, using the Jekyll framework that utilizes Ruby, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
